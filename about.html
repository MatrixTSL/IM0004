About Matrix TSL

Matrix Technology Solutions Ltd is a global provider of hands-on engineering education training solutions. We develop, create and manufacture innovative hardware and software designed to support the teaching of multiple engineering disciplines.

Our Vision

Inspiring the next generation of engineers through practical, hands-on learning that transforms classroom theory and prepares young people for the careers of tomorrow.

Our Mission

At Matrix TSL, our mission is to transform engineering teaching by developing innovative, hands-on solutions that make complex concepts accessible, engaging, and applicable to real-world scenarios with proven results.

Who are we?

We are committed to empowering educators with the resources and support they need to inspire the next generation of engineers and technicians. Through practical hardware, intuitive software, and comprehensive learning materials, we help bridge the gap between theory and practice — ensuring that learners at every level develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in modern engineering and technology industries.

About Halifax

Halifax is a historic market town in West Yorkshire, England, known for its rich industrial heritage and vibrant community. Located in the heart of Yorkshire, Halifax has been a center of commerce and industry for centuries.

Location & Heritage

Halifax is situated in the Calder Valley, surrounded by the beautiful Pennine hills. The town has a proud history of textile manufacturing, engineering, and innovation, making it an ideal location for educational technology companies like Matrix TSL.

Modern Halifax

Today, Halifax continues to be a thriving town with a mix of traditional industries and modern businesses. It serves as an important regional center for education, commerce, and culture in West Yorkshire.

Information about the Product

Product Overview

Model: IM0004 - Industrial Maintenance Closed Loop PID Control System
Product Range: Industrial Maintenance
Curriculum Codes: CP0539 & CP6773
Power Supply: 24V DC

Core Components

Siemens S7-1200 PLC, Unified Basic HMI, turbine flow sensor, proportional control valve, IFM temperature sensor, float switches, proximity sensor, and immersion pump.

Educational Focus

Designed for 16-18 year old engineering students and apprentices in further education, aligned with UK T-Level & BTEC qualifications in Maintenance, Installation & Repair.

Hands-on Training

13+ guided worksheets covering closed-loop control theory, component diagnostics, fault scenarios, and real-world troubleshooting simulations.

Fault Simulation

Built-in software-based faults and hardware fault simulation via removable wiring, including emergency stops, PID setpoint issues, temperature errors, and safety interlocks.

Safety & Standards

CE/UL compliant components, 24V DC operation, comprehensive safety measures, and compliance with educational equipment standards.
